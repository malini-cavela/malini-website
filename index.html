About

Vision into strategy.
Strategy into impact.

Operations Systems Design Supply Chain Management Partnerships Program Management Entrepreneurship Social Innovation Team Leadership Change Management

Malini Srikrishna is an operations executive, founder, and systems builder with a decade of experience turning complex, high-stakes ideas into measurable outcomes. She has led initiatives across education, finance, supply chain, and technology — building the structures that let organisations move from intention to impact.

A Harvard Divinity School graduate and Morrill Scholar, Malini brings a rare combination of analytical rigour and human-centred thinking. She understands that great operations aren't just about efficiency — they're about creating the conditions where people and ideas thrive.

She founded Milan Global — spun out of the Harvard Innovation Labs — to provide business, storytelling, technology, and capacity-building services to impactful startups, organisations, and leaders around the world. She has also served as Vice President of Global Operations at NxGen COACH Network™ and Director of Partnerships at the Harvard Alumni for Global Development.

Across every context she has worked in, the pattern is the same: Malini is the person who makes things actually happen.
